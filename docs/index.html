Bio

Kai W. Li is a programmer and writer who probes the cross sections of language, visual art and code. He is an undergraduate student at UW-Madison who codes (mostly) as much as he writes (eh).

His writing has previously appeared in Litro Magazine and in The Daily Cardinal.

Kai is originally from Montréal, Québec and currently lives in the San Francisco Bay Area.
